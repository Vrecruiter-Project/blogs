Top 5 Website Features Your Customers Expect in 2025

By V recruiters May 1, 2025 5 min read

A glimpse into the future of website design

AI-Powered Chatbots

AI chatbots are revolutionizing customer interactions by providing instant responses, personalized recommendations, and 24/7 support. These chatbots can understand natural language and adapt to customer needs, making them indispensable for modern websites.

  • Instant customer support
  • Personalized recommendations
  • 24/7 availability

Top view of a 2025 planner with wooden letters spelling 'Marco' on a neutral background.

Voice Search Optimization

As voice search becomes more prevalent, websites must be optimized for voice queries. This includes using natural language, answering questions directly, and ensuring content is easily searchable by voice commands.

  • Natural language processing
  • Direct answers to queries
  • Voice command compatibility

Electronic security gate in a modern clothing store entrance for theft prevention.

Augmented Reality (AR) Integration

AR allows customers to visualize products in real-time, enhancing the online shopping experience. From virtual try-ons to interactive product demos, AR is becoming a key feature for engaging users.

  • Virtual product try-ons
  • Interactive demos
  • Enhanced user engagement

Silhouette of a father and child looking through a telescope at Burj Khalifa observation deck.

Dark Mode Compatibility

Dark mode is no longer just a preference; it's a standard feature. Websites that support dark mode offer better readability, reduced eye strain, and improved battery life for mobile users.

  • Reduced eye strain
  • Improved battery life
  • Enhanced readability

A thoughtful portrait of a young black child with braided hair wearing a striped t-shirt.

Personalized Content

Customers expect tailored content based on their preferences and behavior. This includes personalized product recommendations, dynamic pricing, and customized user experiences.

  • Personalized product recommendations
  • Dynamic pricing
  • Customized user experience
